Speed UP
In our game we have two friends stuck in a room in a nuclear power plant and they have to find a way to stop an active alarm which is threatening their lives. They have to get out before it is too late because the place will explode . At first they will start looking for anything that can help them in this darkness.They will find a book on a table which has a floppy disk hidden inside it ,so the players must put this floppy in the pc to begin the game.
